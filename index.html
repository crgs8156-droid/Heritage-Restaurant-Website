Our Story

A Legacy of Taste

Nestled in the heart of Gorabazar, Berhampore, Heritage Restaurant has long been a treasured name among food lovers across Murshidabad. Built on authentic Bengali and Mughlai flavours, every dish carries generations of culinary wisdom.

From our legendary Special Biryani slow-cooked with saffron and secret spices, to our soul-warming Handi Chicken and buttery Special Kulcha, each plate tells a story of heritage. Now we bring that same legacy straight to your doorstep.
